Amazing Cat Facts

Here are some interesting facts about cats that you may not know:

Cats Sleep a Lot

On average, cats spend around 13 to 16 hours a day sleeping. They are crepuscular, meaning they are most active during dawn and dusk.

Cats Can't Taste Sweetness

Unlike humans, cats lack taste receptors for sweetness. They are obligate carnivores, meaning their diet consists mostly of meat.

Whiskers Are Super Sensitive

A cat's whiskers are highly sensitive to touch and help them detect changes in their environment. They use whiskers to measure the width of spaces and to hunt prey.

Cats Have a Strong Sense of Smell

Cats' sense of smell is 14 times stronger than humans! They use their noses to detect food, other animals, and even changes in their environment.

Cats Can Rotate Their Ears 180 Degrees

Cats have 32 muscles in each ear, which allows them to rotate their ears 180 degrees to detect sounds from all directions.
